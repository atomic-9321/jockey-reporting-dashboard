## UK HERO PRODUCTS

### Product 1: Light Lift Seamfree Bra (UK)

**What it is:** Wire-free bralette with light molded cups for gentle lift and natural shaping. Pull-on design, no hardware.

**What it is NOT:** Push-up bra, heavy shaping bra, high-impact support bra, universal fit for all cup sizes.

**Core positioning:** "Light lift that feels effortless — shape without the squeeze, designed for the way you actually want to dress."

**Key features:**
- Light lift molded cups (soft foam, subtle natural shape)
- Sewn-in padding (no bunching, no adjusting after wash)
- Seamfree construction (smooth finish, fewer pressure points)
- Wire-free support (fabric + structure, not metal)
- Narrow, adjustable, convertible straps (normal or criss-cross)

**Safe claims (review-backed):**
- Light lift without compression or squeeze
- Natural shape enhancement for small to medium cups
- Comfortable for long all-day wear (8+ hours tested)
- Seamfree design and smooth silhouette
- Adjustable, convertible straps for outfit flexibility
- Wire-free support without sacrifice
- Works well under tees, strappy tops, fitted dresses
- Sewn-in molded cups (if that's the SKU)

**Unsafe claims (NEVER use):**
- Perfect for every body or all cup sizes
- Ideal for small-breasted women (contradicts evidence)
- Highly stable band (rolls on some shapes)
- Replaces a structured bra
- Cooling or breathable

**Best avatars:** No-Hardware Minimalist, Outfit Workhorse Hunter (strappy/tank focus), Recovery & Sensitivity Seeker

**NOT ideal for:** Full-Bust Spillover Survivor, Band-Betrayal Woman (needs max stability), Heat-Sensitive Adapter

**Key differentiator vs Back Smoothing:** Light Lift = lift + outfit flexibility with narrow straps. Back Smoothing = smoothing + wide back coverage with fixed straps.

**Safe claims (review-backed):**

- Wire-free support that feels more like loungewear

**Unsafe claims (NEVER use):**

- Invisible under all fabrics
- Perfect for every body / all sizes
- High-compression shaping or slimming

**Best avatars:** Band-Betrayal Woman, Wardrobe Gatekeeper, Low-Risk Loyalist, No-Hardware Minimalist, Size-Anxious Returner

**NOT ideal for:** Heat-Sensitive Adapter, Full-Bust Spillover Survivor (uniboob risk)

### Product 5: Classic Full-Rise Y-Front Brief (UK)

**What it is:** Traditional, full-rise men's brief in 100% combed cotton rib with iconic Y-Front fly design and double-lined pouch.

**What it is NOT:** Modern low-rise style, performance athletic brief, compression garment, fashion-forward product.

**Core positioning:** Traditional, reliable everyday comfort for men who want a classic brief that stays soft and lasts years — not months.

**Key features:**
- 100% combed cotton rib fabric (rare in market — hard to find)
- Y-Front fly design (iconic, functional, heritage)
- Double-lined front pouch (support without compression)
- Full-rise cut (sits at belly button / natural waist)
- Contoured leg opening with scalloped elastic waistband
- Proven 5-10 year lifespan per item when QC maintained

**Safe claims (review-backed):**
- Exceptional softness that lasts wash after wash
- 100% cotton — hard to find elsewhere
- Y-Front design provides functional support
- Full-rise stays in place, doesn't ride up
- "Gently hug without wrinkling or sagging but aren't tight"
- 5-10 year durability (when quality control maintained)
- Hot wash capable (60-95°C)
- 20-40+ year customer loyalty relationships

**Unsafe claims (NEVER use):**
- "Perfect fit" or "true to size" (sizing inconsistency crisis in recent batches)
- Modern or trendy
- Performance or athletic
- Suitable for all body types without size guidance

**CRITICAL WARNING — Quality Control:**
Recent batches (last ~2 years) showing sizing inconsistency and extreme shrinkage in some batches. Long-term customers reporting abandonment after decades of loyalty. Sizing guidance MUST be explicit. AI agent should note this context when analyzing performance dips.

**Best avatars:** Multi-Decade Traditionalist (45-85+), Active Support-Seeker/Boxer Convert (25-50), 100% Cotton Purist (30-65), Y-Front Fly Loyalist (40-70), Elderly Loyalist (72-85+)

**What it is NOT:** Full-rise traditional brief, 100% cotton rib heritage product, performance athletic trunk, compression garment, loose boxer short.

**Core positioning:** Reliable everyday comfort with natural cotton feel and stretch flexibility — the trusted brand alternative to fashion-focused trunks at a fair multipack price.

**Key features:**
- 95% cotton / 5% elastane stretch fabric
- Modern trunk cut (mid-rise)
- Branded elastic waistband
- 3-pack format (value positioning)
- Washable at 60°C

**Competitive context:** Competes directly with Calvin Klein, Tommy Hilfiger, Boss, Polo Ralph Lauren in German premium stretch-cotton trunk market. Wins on material comfort, brand trust, and everyday wearability — not fashion prestige.

**Safe claims (review-backed):**
- Soft cotton-dominant fabric with stretch for comfort
- Doesn't ride up or twist
- Good quality for everyday wear
- Better value than fashion brand singles at similar quality
- Works well under slim-fit jeans and business trousers

**Best avatars (DE):** No-Fuss Commuter, Active Dad, Slim-Fit Jeans Guy, Comfort-Seeker, Fashion-Conscious Minimalist, Brand-Trust Believer, Value-Oriented Multipack Shopper, Sensitive Skin Shopper, Fitness-Minded Everyday Guy, Practical Everyday Man

## Product Rules for AI Agent
- If a hero product has no active ads → flag immediately
- If an ad uses an UNSAFE CLAIM for any product → flag as compliance risk
- If performance drops on Y-Front Brief → check if quality/sizing issues are the root cause, not just creative
- Never recommend "cooling" or "breathable" claims for bralettes — contradicts customer evidence
- If comparing Light Lift vs Back Smoothing in insights → use the differentiation table (lift+straps vs smoothing+wide back)
- Always match ad creative angles to the product's approved avatar list
